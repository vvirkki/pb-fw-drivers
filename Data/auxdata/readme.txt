This folder should be filled with auxiliary data from external sources as specified below.

Terrestrial MSA for 2015 by Schipper et al. (2020)

Citation:
Schipper, A. M. et al. Projecting terrestrial biodiversity intactness with GLOBIO 4. Glob. Change Biol. 26, 760–771 (2020).
Data access:
https://www.globio.info/globio-data-downloads

HYDE 3.5 population data

Citation:
Klein Goldewijk, K. HYDE data for the Global Carbon Budget (2025). Utrecht University https://doi.org/10.24416/UU01-F45D44 (2025).
Data access:
https://geo.public.data.uu.nl/vault-hyde/hyde35_c9_apr2025%5B1749214444%5D/original/gbc2025_7apr_base/NetCDF/

HANPP for 2010 by Kastner et al. (2021)

Citation:
Kastner, T. et al. Land use intensification increasingly drives the spatiotemporal patterns of the global human appropriation of net primary production in the last century. Glob. Change Biol. 28, 307–322 (2022).
Data access:
https://zenodo.org/records/7313791
