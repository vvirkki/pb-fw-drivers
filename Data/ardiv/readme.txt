This folder contains areal divisions that are used in the analysis.

Both hybas3 and hybas4 areal divisions are created by combining regional
HydroBASINS shapefiles (standard) into one global-scale GeoPackage.

Original HydroBASINS shapefiles are available at:
https://www.hydrosheds.org/products/hydrobasins

Citation to HydroBASINS data:
Lehner, B. & Grill, G. Global river hydrography and network routing: baseline data and new
approaches to study the world’s large river systems. Hydrol. Process. 27, 2171–2186 (2013).
